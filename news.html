Publication

HiMWA published in Genomics, Proteomics & Bioinformatics

Our latest work, HiMWA, has been published in Genomics, Proteomics & Bioinformatics. HiMWA introduces a hierarchical multiple-wave admixture model that reconstructs realistic population admixture histories by jointly modeling ancient hierarchical admixture and recent multiple waves of gene flow. Applied to Central Asian populations, the method revealed a shared layered demographic history and provides a powerful new framework for studying complex human admixture histories. Dr. Kai Yuan served as a co-corresponding author on this study.
